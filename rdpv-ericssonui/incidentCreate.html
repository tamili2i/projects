If customer called in:

Thank you for calling Service Center. This is (your name). Can I get your name please?

Start off by getting the customer information.

1.  Customer Contact - Name of caller

2.  Contact Phone - Verify with customer. Do not give it to them but have them confirm.

3.  Contact Email - Verify with customer. Do not give it to them but have them confirm.

4.  Customer Contact Notes - Any secondary numbers, hours available, and/or preferred contact info.

5.  Company - Name of company they are calling about.

6.  Product - Ask customer which product are they reporting the incident about and select from the Mediaroom Product drop down list? (See list below)

(Customer name), can you tell me which product this is regarding?

a) Mediaroom

b) Mediaroom Reach

c) MediaFirst

7. At the pop-up, ask the customer which Sub-Product the call is about. If not on the list or the popup does not show, leave it blank and select from the full list in the Sub-Product drop down based on info from the customer says. If the product is Reach, we do not fill in a Sub-Product.

8. Bridge Number - Would you like Ericsson to establish a bridge number or do you have one you would like to use? If so, please provide the phone number and entry code.
